Privacy Policy for ProductiFlow

Last updated: 29 November 2025

Overview

ProductiFlow is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed with privacy as a core principle - all your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect any personal information. All data created and used by ProductiFlow is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This includes:

  • Task lists and descriptions
  • Timer settings and session history
  • Focus time statistics
  • Achievement progress
  • User preferences

Permissions Explanation

ProductiFlow requires the following permissions to function:

  • storage: To save your tasks, timer settings, and statistics locally on your device
  • alarms: To run the Pomodoro timer in the background and manage session transitions
  • notifications: To alert you when timer sessions complete

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any user data to third parties. There are no analytics, tracking, or advertising networks in this extension.

Third-Party Services

ProductiFlow does not use any third-party services, APIs, or external dependencies that would access your data.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you either:

  • Uninstall the extension
  • Manually clear browser data
  • Use the extension's reset functionality

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your information. You can:

  • View all stored data through the extension interface
  • Delete individual tasks or clear all data
  • Remove the extension at any time to delete all associated data

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date and notify users through the extension's update notes.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please create an issue on our GitHub repository.
